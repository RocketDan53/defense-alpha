## Methodology

**Scoring Methodology:**
- **Composite Score:** Weighted sum of 13 signals (SBIR advancement, contract wins, VC raises, agency breadth, risk factors). Higher = stronger momentum.
- **Relevance Score:** Semantic similarity between company R&D portfolio and target technology (0-1 scale).
- **Final Rank:** 65% relevance + 35% normalized composite score.

**Pipeline:**
1. **Semantic Search:** SBIR award titles embedded with `all-MiniLM-L6-v2` sentence-transformer (384-dim vectors). Cosine similarity against technology queries.
2. **Signal Scoring:** Composite score from 14 signal types including SBIR progression, contract wins, VC fundraising, multi-agency interest, and risk indicators (customer concentration, SBIR stalling).
3. **Ranking:** 65% technology relevance + 35% composite signal score.
4. **Filtering:** Startups only, net-positive composite score, at least one positive signal, excluding major defense primes.
