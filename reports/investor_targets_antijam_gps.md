# Investor Targets: Anti-Jam GPS Antenna Technology
**Date:** February 25, 2026
**Query:** Investors in defense GPS/PNT, EW, antenna, UAS, RF companies at $1M-$10M stage

---

## Methodology

Searched 4,715 defense entities with technology overlap (GPS, navigation, EW, anti-jam,
antenna, UAS, drones, RF, sensors, autonomy). Filtered to companies with Reg D filings
between $1M-$10M (comparable stage to a $2.5M raise). RELATEDPERSONS data has not been
downloaded from SEC EDGAR yet - each filing below includes an EDGAR link to look up
the actual investor names on the Form D filing.

**Results:** 804 filings across 460 companies
